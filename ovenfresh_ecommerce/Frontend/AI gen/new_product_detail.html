Product Description

Our Chocolate Truffle Cake is a chocolate lover's dream come true. This decadent cake features three layers of moist chocolate sponge, filled with rich chocolate ganache and decorated with chocolate truffles.

Made with premium Belgian chocolate, this cake delivers an intense chocolate experience that melts in your mouth. The cake is covered in a smooth chocolate ganache and topped with handcrafted chocolate truffles.

Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or any special occasion that calls for a chocolate celebration.

Key Features:
  • Made with premium Belgian chocolate
  • Three layers of moist chocolate sponge
  • Rich chocolate ganache filling
  • Decorated with handcrafted chocolate truffles
  • Available in 6", 8", and 10" sizes

For best taste and texture, consume within 3 days of delivery. Store in refrigerator and bring to room temperature 30 minutes before serving.
