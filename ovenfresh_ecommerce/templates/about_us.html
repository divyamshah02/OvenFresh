About Us

Who Are We?

Every flavor has a story behind it, and so does 'Ovenfresh' have one.

We are mostly into food items (preparing delicacies like cakes, pastries, cookies, chocolates, desserts etc.) Our journey began in 1993 (we came online in 2017), and ever since, we've only kept growing under the leadership of C.E.O Ronak Mehta.

Having started off with preparing delicacies like cakes, pastries, cookies, chocolates, desserts etc., today we have expanded our product category to include Tea/Coffee (at our restaurant), and even flowers (sold online).

What Sets Us Apart?

At 'Ovenfresh' we deviate from the mainstream ways of preparing delicacies. We produce variety in taste using the best ingredients. We follow a strict rule of "no compromise in quality".

Carrying a vast experience in the baking industry, most of our staff members are dedicated and innovative professionals who can be relied upon for, both, creativity and excellence.

Being innovative is a continuous process that never stops! The variety of cakes and pastries that we have, goes into hundreds. Mostly, the cakes at 'Ovenfresh' are eggless, barring a few.
