Our Policies

Refund and Cancellation Policy

Since cakes are perishable goods and made to order, we generally do not provide refunds. However, refunds will be issued in the following cases:

  • If the cake or hamper is received in a damaged condition.
  • If items are missing from the hamper.
  • If the products received are expired.
  • If the cake is stale or has an unpleasant smell.

Note: Customers must provide proof (such as photos) of any issues to qualify for a refund. If a customer claims the cake has an unpleasant smell and has discarded it, we will require the cake to be returned to our cloud kitchen for evidence testing. Refunds will not be considered if the cake has been thrown away.

No refunds will be issued for wrong addresses, unavailable recipients, or other causes beyond our control. Once an order is placed, it cannot be canceled.

Delivery Policy

  • Due to the restricted shelf life and potential for melting, we will contact the recipient to ensure availability at the delivery address. Surprise deliveries are not permitted.
  • We deliver all 365 days of the year (including Sundays and public holidays).
  • Deliveries are limited to locations within Mumbai City, within the toll naka.
  • Please provide a regular street address with nearby landmarks for faster delivery.
  • All deliveries will be executed within the specified time slots. If a delivery is made outside this timeframe, it does not entitle the customer to compensation.
